Why Gemma 4?

The first open model family that brings frontier AI to every construction worker's phone

👁️

Multimodal Vision

E2B/E4B process camera frames natively — no separate vision pipeline. The model sees the missing hard hat directly from the camera feed.

🎤

Native Audio

Workers report hazards verbally in any language. "¡Falta casco en la zona norte!" → Structured alert in English and Spanish. No speech-to-text step needed.

🔧

Function Calling

Native tool use produces typed, structured safety assessments. No more fragile JSON parsing — 98%+ schema compliance guaranteed.

🧠

Thinking Mode

Step-by-step auditable reasoning chain: "I see a worker at height without harness. OSHA 1926.502 requires fall protection above 6 feet. Severity: HIGH."

📱

On-Device Inference

E2B runs entirely on the worker's phone. Zero cloud cost. Zero latency dependency. Works offline. Video never leaves the jobsite.

💰 Economics That Scale

On-device inference eliminates per-query cloud costs. The entire Gemma 4 E2B model runs on the worker's phone — no API calls, no token billing, no cloud dependency.

  • $0/inference — Gemma 4 E2B runs entirely on-device
  • ~$0.02/escalation — Only 5% of detections reach cloud
  • ~$50 fine-tuning cost — One RTX 5090 run
  • $15-50/worker/month — SaaS pricing potential
  • $1.7T market — US construction industry TAM

🔒 Privacy by Design

Video never leaves the construction site unless explicitly escalated through the PPE pipeline. WireGuard encryption on all mesh traffic. HIPAA-compliant for biometric data.

  • All inference on-device by default
  • Worker identifiers anonymized before cloud
  • No PII in logs — anywhere
  • WireGuard mesh encryption (Tailscale)
  • OSHA and union contract compliant
