## Executive Summary

Duchess is a four-tier AI construction safety platform built on AR glasses, companion smartphones, optional local servers, and cloud inference. It uses Gemma 4 models to detect PPE violations, analyze construction hazards, and deliver bilingual (EN/ES) safety alerts in real-time.

**Our thesis**: Construction kills more workers than any other industry. Duchess puts frontier AI on every worker's phone — running 100% on-device, privacy-preserving, bilingual — to save lives. That's the story.

**Total addressable prize pool**: Up to **$80,000** across simultaneous tracks:
- Main Track: $50K (1st place)
- Impact Track: $10K (Safety & Trust) + $10K (Digital Equity & Inclusivity) + $10K (Global Resilience)
- Special Technology Track: $10K (Cactus) + $10K (Unsloth)

## Evaluation Rubric Alignment

### Impact & Vision — 40 points (HIGHEST WEIGHT)

> "How clearly and compellingly does your project address a significant real-world problem?"

**Our story**: 
- Construction is the deadliest industry. 1,056 workers died on US construction sites in 2024.
- PPE non-compliance is the #1 preventable cause. OSHA's "Fatal Four" (falls, struck-by, electrocution, caught-in) are all detectable.
- 30%+ of construction workers are Spanish-speaking. Safety warnings in English only = lives lost.
- Duchess puts frontier AI on every worker's phone. No expensive infrastructure. No cloud dependency. Privacy-preserving.

**Key message**: "Every construction worker deserves an AI safety partner that speaks their language."

### Video Pitch & Storytelling — 30 points

> "How exciting, engaging, and well-produced is the video?"

**Video script concept** (≤3 min):
1. **[0:00-0:30] The Problem**: Construction death statistics. Worker on site without hard hat. Real footage.
2. **[0:30-1:15] The Solution**: Duchess walkthrough — glasses detect → phone confirms with Gemma 4 → alert in English and Spanish → worker puts on hard hat.
3. **[1:15-2:00] The Technology**: Architecture diagram animates. Gemma 4 E2B on-device. Cactus routing. "No cloud required." Unsloth fine-tuned model.
4. **[2:00-2:30] The Impact**: Bilingual alerts. Every worker gets the app. Privacy-first. Scalable to any jobsite.
5. **[2:30-3:00] The Future**: Digital twin integration. Multi-site monitoring. Lives saved counter.

### Technical Depth & Execution — 30 points

> "How innovative is the use of Gemma 4's unique features?"

**Gemma 4 features we use**:
1. **Multimodal vision** (E2B) — Direct PPE detection from camera frames
2. **Native function calling** — Structured safety assessment output, no JSON parsing fragility
3. **Audio input** (E2B) — Voice-based hazard reporting in any language
4. **Thinking mode** — Explainable, auditable reasoning chain for safety decisions
5. **System prompts** — Domain-specialized construction safety instructions
6. **128K context** — Multi-frame temporal reasoning across video sequences
7. **140+ languages** — Bilingual EN/ES with extensibility to all construction workforce languages
8. **Variable resolution** — Adaptive visual token budget based on scene complexity
9. **Apache 2.0** — Commercially deployable, no restrictions

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Real multi-device system** — Most hackathon entries will be single-device demos. We have glasses + phone + cloud working together.
2. **Vision + audio multimodal** — Gemma 4 E2B's native vision replaces our text-only pipeline. Direct PPE detection from camera frames.
3. **Bilingual by design** — Not bolted on. EN/ES is in every string resource, every alert, every prompt.
4. **Privacy-first architecture** — Video never leaves the jobsite unless escalated. WireGuard encryption. HIPAA-compliant.
5. **Cactus-native design** — Our 4-tier architecture literally IS intelligent model routing across device tiers.
6. **Commercially viable** — Apache 2.0 license. Real construction industry problem. $1.7T US construction market.
7. **Lives saved** — 1,056 construction deaths in 2024. This isn't a toy project.
